How it works

HaulPath gives you state-by-state compliance at a glance. Enter your load dimensions, select the states along your route, and instantly see permit requirements, pilot car requirements, and escort rules for each state—so you can plan with confidence and stay legal on every run.

1

Enter load dimensions

Input your load width, height, length, and weight. HaulPath uses your specs to check legal thresholds across all 48 continental states.

2

Select your states

Choose each state you’ll travel through. The app analyzes your route and applies the correct regulations for every jurisdiction.

3

See what’s required

View permit needs, pilot car requirements, and escort rules for each state. All data is available offline when you need it.
